About Our School

Sunrise Public School, established with a vision to provide quality education, is located in the heart of Gaya, Bihar. We believe in nurturing young minds through innovative teaching methods and a supportive learning environment.

Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure ensure that every student receives the attention and resources they need to excel academically and personally.

We follow a holistic approach to education that focuses on academic excellence, character building, and overall personality development.
